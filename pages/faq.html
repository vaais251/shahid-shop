How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental United States. International shipping can take 7-14 business days, depending on the destination and customs processing. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. The tracking information will show the current status and estimated delivery date of your package.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees, taxes, or duties that may be imposed by their country's customs authorities.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Certain items, such as personalized products or intimate apparel, are not eligible for return due to hygiene reasons. Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for complete details.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, log into your account, go to your order history, and select the "Return" option for the relevant order. Follow the prompts to generate a return shipping label. You can also contact our customer service team for assistance with returns.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process the return. After processing, refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your financial institution.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the user icon in the top right corner of our website and selecting "Register." You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. You can also create an account during the checkout process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payment information is securely encrypted and processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never store complete credit card details on our servers. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all products sold on ShopEase are 100% authentic. We source our products directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors to ensure authenticity and quality. We do not sell counterfeit or replica items.

How can I check if an item is in stock?

Product availability is displayed on each product page. If an item is in stock, you'll see the available quantity or simply "In Stock." If an item is out of stock, you'll see "Out of Stock" and may have the option to sign up for restock notifications.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

Many of our products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty information, if applicable, is listed on the product page. Additionally, all products are covered by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee. For warranty claims beyond our return period, please contact the manufacturer directly.

Couldn't Find Your Answer?

If you have a question that isn't answered here, our customer service team is ready to help. Contact us via email, phone, or our contact form.
